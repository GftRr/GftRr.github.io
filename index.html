About Me

I'm Islam Hany, a 17-year-old aspiring cybersecurity specialist and competitive programmer from Egypt 🇪🇬. My journey into tech started completely from scratch — and that's exactly what makes it real.

I began with the fundamentals, moved into web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), then into Python — where I built my foundation in programming and logical thinking. Every step shaped how I think and solve problems.

Right now I'm deep in C++ and algorithms, sharpening my competitive programming skills one problem at a time. I also have a solid grasp of front-end basics and enjoy building clean, structured web interfaces.

Recently I started focusing on cybersecurity — especially the Red Team path. Understanding systems deeply, thinking like an attacker, and constantly pushing myself to learn how things really work under the surface.

My goal is to study at a German university after high school and build an international career in cybersecurity. I believe starting from zero and improving step by step is what builds real strength — and I'm fully committed to that process.
